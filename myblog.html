My Journey Into Technology

Mohammed Fazil Khatib

Hello and welcome to my personal blog. My name is Mohammed Fazil Khatib, and I am a Computer Science diploma student at Tippu Saheed Institution of Technology. I am currently pursuing a Full Stack Development internship at E-Solution, where I am beginning my journey into the world of modern software development.

I consider myself a beginner, but I strongly believe that every expert starts from scratch. This blog is a reflection of my learning path—from understanding basic programming concepts to building real-world applications step by step.

Why I Started This Blog

Technology is constantly evolving, and learning computer science is not just about writing code—it is about developing the right mindset. I created this blog to: Document what I learn from the basics Share my experiences as a student and intern Improve my technical and communication skills Stay consistent and disciplined in my learning journey This space will serve as my learning diary, where I openly share both my progress and challenges.

My Learning Path

As a Full Stack Development intern, I am gradually exploring: Programming fundamentals Web development concepts Front-end and back-end technologies Problem-solving and logical thinking I am learning everything from scratch, focusing on building strong foundations rather than rushing into advanced topics.

My Goals

My goal is to become a skilled and responsible software developer who not only writes code but also understands its purpose and impact. I aim to: Learn continuously Build meaningful projects Gain practical industry experience Contribute positively to the tech community

A Note of Gratitude

Thank you for taking the time to read my blog. I hope this journey inspires other beginners like me to start learning without fear.
